Settlement Network is a settlement capability within the Cobo Payments product. When funds move through the settlement network, Cobo records the resulting balance change with the `SettlementNetwork` source type. You can retrieve these balance changes by calling [List balance changes](/payments/en/api-references/payment/list-balance-changes).

For background on how accounts, balances, and settlement fit together in the Payments product, see [Accounts and fund allocation](/payments/en/guides/amounts-and-balances).
