The Payments AI Assistant is a chatbot embedded in Cobo Portal. It can help you with:
- **Initial setup and configuration** - Bind Cobo Guard and invite team members to your organization
- **Solution design** - Get a complete payment solution tailored to your business needs and use case
- **Questions and troubleshooting** - Learn about Cobo Payments features, APIs, integration workflows, and best practices


<Note>
Cobo AI is currently available in the development environment only.
</Note>


## How to use it

After [signing in to Cobo Portal](https://manuals.cobo.com/en/portal/sign-up), you can open the **AI Assistant** conversation panel from the bottom-left corner of Cobo Portal.

<img src="/payments/en/images/payments/ai-assistant.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="AI Assistant conversation panel"/>


## Data Usage and Security

The Payments AI Assistant is part of Cobo AI. For design principles, data usage and security, and capability boundaries, see [Cobo AI introduction](https://manuals.cobo.com/en/ai/introduction).
